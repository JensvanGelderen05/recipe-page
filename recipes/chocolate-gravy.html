Choclate Gravy

Home Page

Description

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar, or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups hot milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, flour, and cocoa powder together in a saucepan. Stir in 1/2 cup milk slowly. Add egg. Stir in remaining milk. Cook over medium heat until thick, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from heat; add butter and vanilla extract.
